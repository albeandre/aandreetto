Phil will liv.
Farewell present for a former colleague.

Phil is a former colleague in Stylight. He was working in the brand team as graphic designer. A couple of months ago he decided to leave the company to challenge new experiences. Phil is the kind of guy that always makes bad jokes about words. We work in an international environment and is really easy to misunderstand some english/german words. We all knew his passion about this jokes so we decided to ask to everyone to create a joke with his own name (Phil sounds like feel or fill), we did a shooting and we created a booklet as farewell present.
We'll miss you Phil.
